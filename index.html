Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to help you plan your perfect event

How far in advance should I book an event?

We recommend booking your event 2-3 days in advance for guaranteed availability. However, we also accommodate last-minute events based on our schedule. Contact us immediately for urgent bookings.

Do you provide customized menu options?

Absolutely! We offer fully customized menus tailored to your preferences, dietary requirements, and budget. Our experienced team will work with you to create the perfect dining experience for your guests.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Malappuram and surrounding areas in Kerala. Our team is equipped to handle events at any location within our service region with professional on-site management.

What is included in your event planning package?

Our comprehensive packages include event planning, catering, stage décor, lighting, sound management, and full on-site coordination. We customize packages based on your specific needs and budget.

Do you handle dietary restrictions and allergies?

Yes, we take dietary restrictions very seriously. Our certified kitchen team can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-friendly menus. Please inform us in advance about any special requirements.

What is your cancellation and rescheduling policy?

We offer flexible cancellation and rescheduling options. Please contact us for specific details about our policies, which may vary based on event type and booking date.
